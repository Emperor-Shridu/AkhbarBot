# Prompt and Interaction Source of Truth

Active LLM prompts/gems live in:

- `prompts.py`

Active user-facing WhatsApp and Telegram interaction text lives in:

- `user_interactions.py`

Do not add new production prompts or channel copy directly inside webhook handlers or agents. Keep the application methodology limited to:

1. Audio to news.
2. Social video/audio link to news using `yt-dlp` audio extraction.
3. Document/photo OCR to news article.
4. Text to news article.
